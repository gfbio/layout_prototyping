# Landing Page Notes

- Logo near Navi
- 100 % Picture
- Claim (Header/ Subheader or text)
- Link(s) to Action
- Navi transparent on load

#### Or

- Mix above
- claim, action, picture on top
- Navi in mid
- Services in bottom

- Foto "assembled" for landing area
